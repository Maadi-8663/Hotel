Kinara Hotel

Set near the Chenab River, this low-key hotel off National Highway 5 is 35 km from Rachna Wild Life Park and 38 km from Alshafi Palace Ghagoki. The down-to-earth, simply furnished rooms feature Wi-Fi access, TVs, and tea and coffeemaking equipment; some have sitting areas. Upgraded rooms and suites are also available. There are 4 restaurants, 1 of which overlooks the river and offers daily live music. Other amenities include a fitness centre, a beauty salon, a bakery, and an amusement park for children. There are also guest laundry services, and meeting and event space for up to 5,000 people.
