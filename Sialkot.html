HOTELS OF SIALKOT

Welcome to the Hotels of Sialkot. Thanks for visiting us. We provide you all the necessary information you require. Here you can find the famous hotels of Sialkot.




Hotel de RAJ

Located among shops and eateries, this relaxed hotel is 5 km from both the Sialkot Clock Tower and Sialkot Fort. It’s 15 km from Sialkot International Airport. Simply furnished rooms and suites provide Wi-Fi and TVs, plus minifridges. Room service is available 24/7. There’s a warmly decorated restaurant, and event space. A breakfast buffet is available.

Hotel the Jeevens

Set on a busy road near bank and university branches in Zeeshan Colony, this polished hotel is 6 km from the historic Sialkot Fort and 3 km from Fatima Jinnah Park. It's 19 km from Sialkot International Airport. The straightforward rooms, some with sofabeds and city views, have Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. Upgraded suites add jetted showers and extra bedrooms; 1 has ornate furniture and a balcony. The 2 restaurants serve pizza, and Chinese and Thai fusion cuisine. A gym includes an indoor pool, sauna and hot tub. There's rhrefined meeting and banquet space for up to 700 people. A breakfast buffet is available. To go there click on "location".

Location

Hotel Javson

In an elegant building along a main road, this polished hotel is 3 km from the city centre, 6 km from the ancient Sialkot Fort and 17 km from Sialkot International Airport. The genteel, warmly decorated rooms provide Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, plus tea and coffeemaking facilities. Suites add sitting areas. There's an informal international restaurant with a bar. Other amenities include a gym and an indoor pool, as well as a spa area with a hot tub, a sauna and a steam bath. A breakfast buffet is available. To reach there, click on the "location".
